Counterpoint 360° Club

For years, Counterpoint Edu Lab has been shaping young minds globally with the Counterpoint 360° Club, an innovative and customizable online/offline training experience for students under 18. Developed by our award-winning team of coaches and educators, this immersive platform seamlessly integrates the analytical precision of debating with the creativity of theatre-based improvisation. Through a hands-on approach that includes multimedia tools, physical activities, and thought experiments, we engage students in a holistic development process that encourages out-of-the-box thinking and clear expression.

The Counterpoint 360° Club, adaptable for schools, local communities, and student groups, culminates in a dynamic showcase event where students display their refined skills under expert guidance. This enriching journey has captivated students worldwide, many of whom are now flourishing in diverse professional fields. Join the Counterpoint 360° Club and become part of a distinguished tradition of world class debating, where logical dexterity and creative spirit come together, celebrating those who think and introspect at every turn

School Debate Club

Counterpoint Edu Lab proudly presents the School Debate Club, a bespoke educational experience tailored for schools. Designed to span the academic year, this 24-module program merges the analytical depth of debating with the creative flair of theatre-based improvisation. Each session is crafted by our award-winning team of coaches and educators to engage students through multimedia tools, physical activities, and thought experiments, nurturing their ability to think critically and express themselves clearly.

Throughout the year, students will embark on a transformative journey, culminating in an exciting showcase event where they will exhibit their newly acquired skills under expert guidance. The School Debate Club has inspired countless students, helping them thrive in their academic pursuits and beyond. By joining, schools become part of an esteemed tradition of fostering comprehensive skill development, where students' analytical minds and creative spirits are cultivated to their fullest potential.

Enrichment Camp

Get ready for the Counterpoint Enrichment Camp, an action-packed five to ten-day adventure that blends debate, public speaking, theatre, team-building, and some friendly competition. Dive into exciting activities like scavenger hunts, collaborative projects, and hands-on learning that will arm you with skills to last a lifetime.

Held throughout the year and open to school students worldwide, our camps have been transforming young minds since 2014, with some of today’s trainers starting as campers. Located in various scenic and historic venues globally, the camp offers unforgettable experiences no matter the season.

What sets us apart is that we like to move and groove, the camp is not limited to modish debate crowds prattling in debate jargon but also includes thrilling trips and outdoor adventures. Explore the Ellora Caves, trek through lush landscapes, plant rice in Assam, or scale rock faces. Engage in intellectually stimulating activities such as archaeological digs, art curation, and cultural studies. Participate in mock parliaments that reflect the local governance of the camp’s location and take part in eco-friendly projects like beach clean-ups or reforestation. The Counterpoint Enrichment Camp is your ticket to a season of fun, learning, and discovery!

Counterpoint Boot Camp

The Counterpoint Boot Camp is a dynamic 3 to 5 day immersive experience that takes learning beyond the classroom and into the realm of real-world application. Combining intensive debate training with interactive activities, this bootcamp equips students with critical thinking, public speaking, and teamwork skills in a fun and engaging environment.

From creative theatre exercises to thought-provoking debates, participants will dive into group challenges, scavenger hunts, and collaborative projects designed to build confidence and foster personal growth. With a history of successful camps across India's major cities, the Counterpoint Boot Camp ensures students leave with not only refined oratory skills but also lasting memories and friendships.

The Counterpoint Boot Camp delivers an all-encompassing, hands-on experience that empowers students to dive into debates with bold confidence and unleash their creative potential. It's more than just a learning journey—it's an adventure that leaves them inspired, energized, and ready to conquer the world of debate!

WSC Training

At Counterpoint Edu Lab, we have a longstanding commitment to promoting critical thinking and public speaking within the Indian education sector. Since 2013, we have dedicated ourselves to training students for various editions of the World Scholar’s Cup Championships, helping them achieve extraordinary success.

Remarkable Milestones
  • In 2013, we celebrated our first major success with one of our students being named the Top Debater, securing the fourth individual position and ranking as the 10th best debater at the ToC.
  • In 2014, our head coach, Salil Singh, was honored as the WSC Coach of the Year. That same year, three of our teams ranked among the top 15 debate teams at the ToC.
  • Our journey continued with an overall ninth-place finish at the ToC in 2015, and in 2016, our team proudly secured the second place at the WSC Globals.
  • Between 2016 and 2019, we trained delegations from prestigious schools such as Pathways School Noida and Gurgaon, GD Goenka Sarita Vihar, Heritage School Vasant Kunj, Presidium Gurgaon, IPS Indirapuram, and many more, consistently producing competitive teams each season.
  • Post-Covid, we returned stronger, with our senior debate team achieving the top senior debate team status at the Globals in 2022 and our junior debate team securing the top debate spot in 2023.
Our Approach

Our training sessions are meticulously tailored to meet the diverse needs of students, providing them with the necessary tools and skills to excel in competitive environments. By conducting numerous coaching sessions across various schools, we ensure that our students are well-prepared to tackle the challenges of WSC and other prestigious debating competitions such as the ISDS and WSDC amongst others.

Counterpoint Edu Lab continues to be a beacon of excellence in debate training, fostering a culture of methodical thinking and effective communication among students. Our legacy of success and commitment to education remains unwavering, as we strive to equip the next generation of debaters with the skills they need to shine on the global stage.
