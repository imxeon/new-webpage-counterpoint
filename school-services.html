School Services

Audits

Our comprehensive audit services help schools identify areas of improvement and maintain high-quality standards. The School Systems Audit provides a detailed evaluation of all operational systems within the school, ensuring they are efficient and effective. The Teachers’ Skill Audit focuses on analyzing teachers' skills and competencies, highlighting strengths and areas for professional growth. Additionally, our School Quality Assurance Audit assesses various aspects of school operations to guarantee adherence to established quality standards.

Trainings

We offer a range of training programs to empower educators and administrative staff. Our Teacher Training programs are aligned with the National Curriculum Framework, enhancing teaching methodologies and classroom practices. We support the Development of Pedagogical Tools, providing guidance on creating innovative teaching aids to make learning more engaging. Our experts also assist in developing Assessment Tools, such as rubrics and portfolios, for a holistic approach to student evaluation. Additionally, we offer Holistic Progressive Cards Training to implement comprehensive student report cards covering academic, social, and emotional growth. Our School Quality Assurance Framework training ensures schools can effectively apply quality standards, while our Administrative Departments Training provides professional development for school administrative staff, enhancing their efficiency and effectiveness.

Recruitment Support

Finding the right talent is crucial for a school's success, and our recruitment support services help you build a competent team. We assist in Teacher Selection, identifying and hiring qualified teachers that align with your school's specific needs. Our support extends to Administrative Staff Recruitment, ensuring you find capable personnel to manage school operations efficiently. For leadership roles, we offer Guidance in Selecting School Leaders who have the vision and skills to drive positive change and lead the school toward excellence.

School Quality Assurance

We are committed to helping schools maintain and enhance their quality standards. Our Implementation Guidance provides expert support in applying the School Quality Assessment and Assurance Framework, enabling schools to foster continuous improvement. We also assist in Self-Assessment processes, empowering schools to conduct internal quality checks and ensure ongoing adherence to educational standards, thereby promoting a culture of excellence.

Policy and Advocacy

Well-defined policies are the backbone of a successful school. We offer Policy and Advocacy services to help schools develop a strong academic and operational framework. Our School Processes Policies service provides expert guidance in drafting policies that align with your school's vision and mission, covering various aspects of school operations. Additionally, we assist in creating Academic Policies, including comprehensive assessment and curriculum policies, to ensure a consistent and effective learning experience for students.
