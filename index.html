Who we are?

We are an education company working to promote collaboration, creativity, critical analysis and interdisciplinary thinking through extra-curricular programs, school curriculum-building and enrichment camps across India.

Our founder, Salil Singh, started coaching school debaters in his teens for pocket money and after coaching hundreds of schools and colleges with an ever growing team, conceptualized Counterpoint Edulabs in 2018 to give a shape to our already immense experience in the field and a canvas to our projects of the future. We have trained students from 600+ schools in our journey so far.

Our team is made up of curriculum experts, debate and theater coaches, debaters quizzers and educators who work together to create modules and immersive learning experiences tailor made for our learners.
